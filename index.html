What We Do

For me, photography is more than a way of making memories - it is an art form which allows me to express myself, with the only boundaries being the ones which I create.

When I make a piece, I allow the viewer to come into my space and go on a journey with me. A degree in photography will allow me to develop the ability to confidently explore my ideas, make contextual links and references to artists and photographers, until it all comes together in a final piece which expresses those ideas in a sophisticated way.
